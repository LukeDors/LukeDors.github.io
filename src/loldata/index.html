League of Legends Data Analysis Overview

For this project I have begun gathering game data for all champions in League of Legends on their most decicated and highest ranked players (OTPs). As a result, I will have the most recent games from various sites (lolalytics.com, u.gg, op.gg) from the top 50 OTPs from each major region (EUW, KR, NA), and I may include LAS as well. Currently, I have collected data from Galio OTPs to get a general sense of the data I will be working with. I am using MongoDB for this, as I want the ease of working with specific collections, although I will have to later restructure much of this into standard tabular dataframes later in the project. My goals for this data are:

• Display what top OTPs build for various matchups

• Identify any differences between champs in other regions

• Discover which champions are the most feast/famine

• Find the best bans based on top players for each champion

• Highlight the biggest differences between OTP winrates and non-OTP winrates (skill floor/ceiling)

• Show which champions scale the best comparatively

• Find ideal champion pools given a single champion

• Create a machine learning model which predicts game outcomes at team preview
